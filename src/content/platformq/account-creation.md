# Account Creation

This guide explains how to create and set up your PlatformQ account.

## Creating an Account

1. **Visit the Registration Page**
   - Go to [platformq.microbin.io/register](https://platformq.microbin.io/register)
   - Click the "Create Account" button

2. **Enter Your Information**
   - Provide your email address
   - Create a secure password
   - Fill in your personal and company information

3. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your inbox for a verification email
   - Click the verification link to activate your account

4. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add your professional details
   - Set up your preferences
   - Configure notification settings

## Account Types

PlatformQ offers several account types to suit different needs:

- **Individual**: For freelancers and individual developers
- **SMB**: For small and medium-sized businesses
- **Enterprise**: For large organizations with multiple teams
- **Educational**: For academic institutions and students
