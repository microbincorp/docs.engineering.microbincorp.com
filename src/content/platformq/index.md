# PlatformQ Documentation

Welcome to the PlatformQ documentation. PlatformQ is an AI-enabled instant design & development project management platform for OEMs and SMBs to utilize MicroBin Corporation's services and solutions.

## What is PlatformQ?

PlatformQ is a comprehensive project management platform that leverages artificial intelligence to streamline the design and development process. It helps organizations efficiently utilize MicroBin Corporation's services and solutions, reducing time-to-market and development costs.

## Key Features

- AI-powered design assistance
- Integrated development environment
- Automated testing and validation
- Seamless integration with MicroBin hardware
- Collaborative project management tools
- Supply chain optimization
