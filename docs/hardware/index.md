# Hardware Documentation

Welcome to the MicroBin Hardware documentation. Here you'll find comprehensive guides and documentation to help you work with MicroBin's hardware products.

## Overview

MicroBin Corporation offers a wide range of commercial off-the-shelf hardware products designed for various applications. Our product families include:

- **Per4mer Series**: High-performance computing solutions
- **Ex6eeder Series**: Extended capability modules and systems
- **Excep7ional Series**: Premium tier hardware solutions

## Product Categories

### Single Board Computers
Our single board computers provide complete computing solutions on a single circuit board.

### System on Modules
Compact computing modules designed to be integrated into custom carrier boards.

### I/O Boards
Expansion boards for additional input/output capabilities.

### Development Kits
Complete kits for rapid prototyping and development.

### Infinite Board Computers
Our most advanced computing platform with unparalleled expandability.
